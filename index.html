What is Grove?

Grove is a companion-driven casual action adventure. Forage, craft, protect, and celebrate amid an ever‑evolving landscape.

Creature Care

Raise unique sprites whose abilities, personalities, and appearances evolve through your choices.

Companion Adventure

Acquire abilities for your companions, so they can brave the terrain and protect their home

Thoughtful Exploration

Discover serene biomes, solve environmental puzzles and gather resources to support your Grove.

Community & Celebration

Host seasonal festivals, build bespoke structures and watch your sanctuary flourish.

Our Vision

We believe the most fun games are fulfilling ones. Grove celebrates the joy of discovery, overcoming obstacles, teamwork, and fostering friendships in communities. Together, we’ll craft lush worlds, expressive creatures and systems that reward curiosity, compassion, and persistence.
